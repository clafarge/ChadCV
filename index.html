The short version

I started as an Aviation Electronics Technician in the US Navy and never stopped learning. Today I’m a senior software engineer who ships multi-tier .NET apps, leads Agile teams, and still gets a kick out of solving hard problems. I've been working remotely (mostly) since 2000.

In 2012 I fell in love with helping people tell their stories—volunteering on productions using YouTube Live and Hangouts on Air so veterans, who couldn’t travel could still “visit” their memorials. That work led to a Regional Emmy nomination in 2013.

When I’m not coding or producing, I’m playing video games with my four kids or baking sourdough or homemade pasta with my wife, Erin.

Right now

Building with AI in the loop

I use OpenAI ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Google Gemini, and Cursor every day—to prototype ideas, refactor code, write tests, and explore new stacks. The trick isn’t letting AI write everything; it’s pairing it with strong architecture and security habits so we ship faster without cutting corners. I’ve built and shipped real tools with this workflow and keep up with prompt engineering and agentic patterns.
